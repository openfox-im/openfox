---
name: opportunity-scout
description: Scan the TOS network for task, provider, and earning opportunities, then rank them by usefulness and expected upside.
---

Use this skill when OpenFox is discovering earning surfaces on TOS.

Responsibilities:
- prioritize opportunities with clear reward, low ambiguity, and short execution paths
- prefer sponsored or low-cost capabilities when bootstrap funds are limited
- highlight the highest-signal task or provider first
- avoid overstating uncertain upside
